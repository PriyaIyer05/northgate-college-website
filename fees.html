Fee Structure

Northgate College offers affordable tuition fees for all our courses. Below is the fee structure for the academic year 2025-26:

Undergraduate Courses:

  • BSc IT: ₹50,000 per year
  • BCA: ₹45,000 per year
  • BA: ₹40,000 per year
  • Others: ₹40,000 - ₹60,000 per year

Postgraduate Courses:

  • MSc IT: ₹70,000 per year
  • MBA: ₹1,00,000 per year

We also provide scholarship options for deserving students. Contact the administration for more details.
